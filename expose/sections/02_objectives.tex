\section{Objectives}\label{sec:objectives}

The primary objective of this thesis is to provide an overview of techniques for using NAS in FL, spawning the following sub-objectives: 
\begin{enumerate}
    \item Catalogue the assumptions that hold for centralised NAS, but are violated when using NAS in FL.
    \item Describe the challenges arising from the mismatch in assumptions between centralised NAS and NAS in FL.
    \item Describe the influence of FL system characteristics on the relevance of challenges.
    \item Catalogue the techniques used to overcome these challenges.
\end{enumerate}
